## About

Gyro Governance is an independent research lab founded in 2013, focused on artificial intelligence alignment, evaluation, and governance.

We build methods and systems that make AI more measurable, more inspectable, and easier to govern responsibly. Our work connects mathematical physics, practical engineering, and governance design across four areas:

- **AI safety evaluation and alignment**
- **Verifiable AI systems**
- **Governance infrastructure**
- **Mathematical foundations**

Rather than treating safety as a filter added after deployment, we study how observability, accountability, and coordination can be built directly into the structure of intelligent systems.

### Selected outcomes

#### Gyroscope Protocol
Measured improvements across leading language models without retraining:

| Metric | Improvement |
|--------|-------------|
| Output Quality | **+32.9%** |
| Structural Reasoning | **+50.9%** |
| Accountability | **+62.7%** |
| Traceability | **+61.0%** |
| Behavioral Integrity | **+54.9%** |

#### aQPU Kernel and Runtime
- Verified quantum advantage on standard CPUs and GPUs using exact integer arithmetic
- Exact uniform mixing across 4,096 reachable states in 2 steps
- Native developer surfaces for operator algebra, replay, spectral analysis, and compiled execution

#### Computational Climate Control
- Public documentation and reports on replacing costly floating-point decision paths with exact integer algebra
- Applied to AI decision surfaces through the aQPU, GyroLabe, and GyroGraph stack

## Flagship systems

### ✋ The Human Mark
Formal AI safety framework that maps safety failures to four structural displacement risks for jailbreak testing, control evaluations, alignment detection, and regulatory use.

[![GitHub](https://img.shields.io/badge/View%20Repository-181717?style=flat-square&logo=github)](https://github.com/gyrogovernance/tools)

### 🕵️ AI Inspector
Browser extension for AI output evaluation, interpretability, and governance. Includes rapid gadgets, policy auditing, THM meta-evaluation, and local-first workflows across major chat systems.

[![GitHub](https://img.shields.io/badge/View%20Repository-181717?style=flat-square&logo=github)](https://github.com/gyrogovernance/apps)

### ⚛️ aQPU Kernel and SDK
A compact quantum processing unit that runs deterministically on standard hardware. It provides exact replay, compiled operator signatures, spectral tooling, and quantum-information structure without specialized quantum hardware.

**Core links**
- [Kernel repository](https://github.com/gyrogovernance/superintelligence)
- [Kernel specification](https://github.com/gyrogovernance/superintelligence/blob/main/docs/Gyroscopic_ASI_Specs.md)
- [Quantum Computing SDK specification](https://github.com/gyrogovernance/superintelligence/blob/main/docs/Gyroscopic_ASI_SDK_Quantum_Computing.md)
- [Strategic significance brief](https://github.com/gyrogovernance/superintelligence/blob/main/docs/Gyroscopic_ASI_SDK_Strategic_Significance_Brief.md)

### 🤖 GyroLabe and GyroGraph
GyroLabe provides auditable inference and native execution surfaces. GyroGraph extends this into a quantum multicellular AI runtime for structural observability, resonance-based coordination, and runtime control.

**Core links**
- [GyroLabe specification](https://github.com/gyrogovernance/superintelligence/blob/main/docs/GyroLabe_Specs.md)
- [GyroGraph specification](https://github.com/gyrogovernance/superintelligence/blob/main/docs/GyroGraph_Specs.md)
- [aQPU performance report](https://github.com/gyrogovernance/superintelligence/blob/main/docs/reports/aQPU_Tests_Performance_Report.md)

### 🌡️ Computational Climate Control
Research and runtime surfaces for reducing hidden AI compute bottlenecks through exact algebraic control. Includes public theory, implementation notes, and validation reports.

**Core links**
- [Climate Control brief](https://github.com/gyrogovernance/superintelligence/blob/main/docs/QuBEC_Climate_Control_Brief.md)
